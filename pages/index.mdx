# What is ApeChain?
ApeChain is a dedicated infrastructure layer to power the ApeCoin ecosystem.
By leveraging Caldera and Arbitrum's custom native token feature, ApeChain will utilize $APE as its native gas token. This integration significantly enhances $APE's utility, fostering a robust and dynamic economy driven by $APE, and positioning ApeChain as a premier platform.
