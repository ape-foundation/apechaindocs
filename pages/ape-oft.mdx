
# Overview of LayerZero's OFT Standard: 

The [Omnichain Fungible Token (OFT) Standard](https://docs.layerzero.network/v2/developers/evm/onft/quickstart) enables fungible tokens to exist across multiple blockchains while maintaining a unified supply. The OFT standard works by debiting an amount of tokens from a sender on the source chain and crediting the same amount of tokens to a receiver on the destination chain. 
$APE, the native gas currency of ApeChain network, is deployed across multiple blockchains using LayerZero's OFT standard. At the moment, the $APE OFT token is available on the following networks:

- ApeChain
- Ethereum
- Arbitrum
- Solana
- HyperEVM
- Binance Smart Chain
